Get the help your family needs.

  • Use our interactive map to search for food pantries and meal sites or school meal pickup sites in your area.
  • Apply for food stamps in Maine by creating an account on My Maine Connection.
  • Get information on recent changes to food assistance programs by visiting Maine Equal Justice.
  • Please follow the CDC's guidelines for what to do if you may have contracted COVID-19 if you or somebody you've had recent contact with feels sick.

About the data

We recommend that you call each pantry before going to pick up food. This data is validated and updated by volunteers, but it can change quickly.
Follow all social distancing guidelines and the medical advice provided by the CDC when picking up food.
