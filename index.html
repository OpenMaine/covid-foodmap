Get the help your family needs.

Apply for food stamps in Maine by creating an account on My Maine Connection. You can also find information on recent changes to food assistance programs by visiting Maine Equal Justice.

About the data

We recommend that you call each pantry before going to pick up food. This data is validated and updated by volunteers, but it can change quickly. Follow all social distancing guidelines and the medical advice provided by the CDC when picking up food.
